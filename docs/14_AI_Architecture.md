# AI Architecture

AI capabilities will be introduced after the transactional platform is stable.

## Principles

- AI augments explicit product workflows; it does not replace domain rules.
- Providers are accessed through application-owned interfaces.
- Prompts, model versions, cost, latency, and quality are observable.
- Personal or sensitive data is minimized and protected.
- High-impact actions require validation and, where appropriate, human approval.

Detailed model and retrieval decisions require ADRs before implementation.
