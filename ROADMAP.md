# RISE Master Roadmap

## Objective

Deliver a production-ready AI marketplace with enterprise engineering standards.

## Phase 1 — Foundation

Status: Complete

- Authentication
- Users
- Geography
- 1stKings Trust
- Categories

## Phase 2 — Marketplace Core

Status: In Progress

- Stores
- Products
- Services
- Reviews
- Media Uploads

Goal: a seller can create a professional storefront with products and services.

## Phase 3 — Marketplace Experience

- Search
- Favorites
- Messaging
- Notifications
- Nearby Services
- Ratings

Goal: buyers can discover and interact with sellers easily.

## Phase 4 — Commerce

- Cart
- Orders
- Payments
- Delivery Tracking
- Invoices

Goal: support end-to-end transactions.

## Phase 5 — Business Intelligence

- Seller Dashboard
- Analytics
- Revenue
- Reports

## Phase 6 — AI Platform

- Seller Assistant
- Buyer Assistant
- Product Generator
- Smart Search
- Fraud Detection
- Recommendation Engine

AI should be built on top of a stable marketplace, not before it.

## Phase 7 — Enterprise

- Multi-country
- Multi-language
- Multi-currency
- Tax Engine
- API Platform

## Phase 8 — RISE v1.0
