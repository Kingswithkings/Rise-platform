# AI Roadmap

AI should be introduced progressively after the core marketplace flows are stable.

## Seller AI

- Generate store descriptions
- Generate product descriptions
- Improve SEO titles
- Pricing suggestions

## Buyer AI

- Conversational marketplace assistant
- Smart product recommendations
- Location-aware service discovery

## Platform AI

- Fraud detection
- Duplicate listing detection
- Search ranking improvements
- Trust score insights

## Principle

AI augments the marketplace. It does not replace explicit business rules, Trust rules, or admin controls.
