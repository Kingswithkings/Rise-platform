# ADR-005 — Use a Modular Monolith

## Context

RISE needs rapid cross-domain delivery without taking on premature distributed-system
complexity.

## Decision

Use a modular monolith for v1.0. Business domains remain isolated inside one FastAPI
application.

## Alternatives considered

Microservices from inception and a structure without enforced domain boundaries.

## Consequences

Deployment and transactions remain simple. Module boundaries require active enforcement,
and domains can be extracted later when evidence justifies the cost.
