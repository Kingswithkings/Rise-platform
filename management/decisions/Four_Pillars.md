# The Four Pillars of RISE

Everything we build must support one of these pillars.

## 1. Marketplace

The marketplace engine.

- Regions
- Countries
- Cities
- Categories
- Stores
- Products
- Services
- Orders
- Payments

## 2. Trust

The trust engine.

- Trust Profiles
- Trust Passport
- Verification
- Reviews
- Risk
- Fraud
- Badges

## 3. AI

The intelligence engine.

- AI Search
- AI Recommendations
- Seller Assistant
- Buyer Assistant
- Business Insights

## 4. Platform

Reusable services.

- Identity
- Payments
- Notifications
- Analytics
- Search
- Maps
- Files

## Rule

Every feature, task, and domain must map to one of these four pillars.
