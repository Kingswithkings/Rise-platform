# Product Requirements Document

## Product

RISE is a multi-sided marketplace for customers, sellers, and service providers.

## Core capabilities

1. Account and identity management
2. Regional marketplace discovery
3. Store, product, and service management
4. Orders and payments
5. Buyer-seller messaging
6. Reviews and moderation
7. Analytics and administration
8. AI-assisted experiences

Feature-level requirements and acceptance criteria belong in the associated task file.
