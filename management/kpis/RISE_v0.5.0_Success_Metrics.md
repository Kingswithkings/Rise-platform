# RISE v0.5.0 Success Metrics

We will know we are on track if, by RISE v0.5.0:

- Users can register and log in.
- Sellers can create stores.
- Sellers can list products and services.
- Buyers can browse by region, country, city, and category.
- Buyers can see 1stKings Trust badges.
- Buyers can search the marketplace.
- The platform is fully documented and deployable.
