# ADR-006 — Event-Driven Reputation

## Title

Derive the 1stKings Trust Score from Trust Events

## Status

Accepted

## Context

A stored score hides why reputation changed and risks drifting from the evidence that
created it.

## Decision

Trust Events are the source of truth. Calculate the bounded 1stKings Trust Score from the
event ledger whenever it is requested. Do not persist the calculated score on a Trust
Profile.

## Alternatives

- Persist only the latest score
- Persist a score and periodically reconcile it

## Consequences

- Every score is explainable and reproducible.
- Fraud or dispute adjustments are represented as new events.
- Read cost grows with event history and may later require a rebuildable projection or
  cache.
- Event creation must be idempotent and access-controlled.
