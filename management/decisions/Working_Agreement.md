# Working Agreement

## Purpose

Keep RISE delivery focused, predictable, and production-oriented.

## Agreement

- The Technical Lead provides design before implementation.
- We complete one domain before starting the next.
- We keep documentation synchronized with the code.
- We review each sprint before moving on.
- We avoid adding new platform ideas unless they support the current sprint.

## Completion Rule

No domain is considered complete until its code, tests, documentation, and migration are all finished.
