# Architecture Principles

These are the engineering principles for RISE.

## Principles

- Business domains first — organize by business capability, not technical layer.
- API-first — web, mobile, and future clients use the same backend.
- Modular monolith — simple now, extract services only when justified.
- Documentation is part of the feature — incomplete documentation means incomplete work.
- Security by design — authentication, authorization, and auditing are built in.
- Observability — logging, metrics, and health checks are standard.
- Testability — core business logic should be covered by automated tests.
- One completed domain at a time — finish code, tests, documentation, and migration before moving on.
